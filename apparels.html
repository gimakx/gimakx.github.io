Your Questions Answered

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I get custom logos and branding on academy apparel?

Yes, absolutely. Since we handle and control every step of production, Gimakx provides 100% customization — your academy logo, design, and color details applied exactly where you want them. GiStrix T-shirts are available with full sublimation, screen print, and embroidery. GiFlex Hoodies support embroidery, appliqué, and full-front print. Every piece in your order carries consistent, uniform branding across the entire run.

Do you allow private labeling on academy apparel?

Yes — we attach your own custom neck labels, size tags, woven patches, and branded packaging so the final product looks 100% yours. Your academy brand is the only brand on the garment.

What is the minimum order quantity?

Minimum order is 10 units per design for both GiStrix T-shirts and GiFlex Hoodies. This applies to each individual design — if you want two colorways, each requires a minimum of 10 units.

How long does production take?

Typical turnaround for custom apparel orders is 3–4 weeks from order confirmation, depending on complexity and quantity. All lead times are confirmed at the time of order.

Can we order apparel together with Gis and rash guards as one bundle?

Absolutely. Gimakx acts as your one-stop uniform partner. A full academy bundle can include BJJ Gis, rash guards, T-shirts, hoodies, belts, patches, bags, and caps — all branded consistently to your academy's identity and shipped together in one order.
