Your Questions Answered

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I get a custom designed rash guard for my academy?

Yes — Evosoft rash guards can be fully sublimated with your academy's logo, sponsors, and color themes. Because we use edge-to-edge sublimation printing, your design runs across the entire garment with no limits on color count or placement.

Are the rash guards suitable for both No-Gi and under-Gi training?

Definitely. The Evosoft is a true dual-purpose rash guard. It's made from 240gsm moisture-wicking, anti-bacterial, and stretchable fabric — ideal for No-Gi sessions as standalone competition gear, and designed to sit flat under the Gi without bunching or causing discomfort during rolls.

Will the rash guard design fade or peel after washing?

No. We use high-quality sublimation printing that permanently bonds the design into the fabric at a molecular level. The colors won't crack, peel, or fade — even after years of regular washing and hard training. This is the fundamental advantage of sublimation over screen printing or heat transfer.

What is the minimum order for Evosoft rash guards?

The minimum order is 10 units per design for the Evosoft custom series. This applies to each individual design — so if your academy wants two different color schemes, each design requires a minimum of 10 units.

Can the Evosoft be matched to our GiPro Gi order?

Absolutely. One of the primary reasons academies choose Gimakx is the ability to coordinate a complete kit from one manufacturer. Your Evosoft rash guard can be designed to match your GiPro Gi in colors, logo placement, and branding — so every athlete wears a unified academy identity from Gi to rash guard.
