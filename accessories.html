Your Questions Answered

Frequently Asked Questions

Can you print academy branding on Gi bags?

Yes — we print your academy logo on Gi bags for a complete branded package. Gi bags are a popular add-on for academies ordering full uniform sets, keeping every element of your kit — from Gi to bag — consistently branded to your academy's identity.

Can I get fully custom badge and patch designs?

Yes. Gimakx provides 100% customization over badge and patch design — any shape, any size, any color count. Woven, embroidered, and PVC rubber constructions are available with your choice of iron-on, sew-on, or hook-and-loop backing, and merrowed or laser-cut borders.

Can belts be customized with academy branding?

Yes — custom embroidery is available on all belt ranks, with rank bar options included. This is particularly popular for grading events and new-student kit distributions where consistency across your academy matters.

Can we order a complete academy kit — Gis, apparel, and accessories — from one supplier?

Absolutely. Gimakx acts as your one-stop uniform partner. A complete academy bundle can include BJJ Gis, rash guards, T-shirts, hoodies, belts, patches, bags, and caps — all branded consistently to your academy's identity and shipped together in one order.
