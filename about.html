Our Craft

At Gimakx we handle full in-house production at our factory in Pakistan — from cutting to final stitching and quality checks. We use premium pre-shrunk pearl weave cotton for durability, as well as 100% pure cotton, across three fabric weights: 380gsm, 450gsm, and 500gsm. Every Gi has its stress points — shoulders, knees, and collar — triple-stitched and reinforced with high-tensile thread for maximum durability. Our standard cuts and fabrics follow IBJJF regulations for sleeve length, patch placement, and fabric type.

What We Make

From competition-ready BJJ Gis to fully sublimated rash guards and complete academy apparel, Gimakx is a one-stop uniform partner. Our range includes the GiPro series of BJJ Gis, Evosoft rash guards, T-shirts and hoodies, and belts, badges, and Gi bags — available in a full size range from M0 to A5, including youth and women's sizes.

Built For Academies

We provide 100% customization over embroidery, patches, and sublimation — with your academy logo, desired placement, and colour details. Through private labeling — custom neck labels, size tags, woven patches, and branded packaging — your product looks 100% yours. With a low minimum order of just 10 units per design, academies of every size can build a consistent, professional identity on the mats.
