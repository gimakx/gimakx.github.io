Our journey began with a shared love for the art of Jiu-Jitsu and a desire to enhance the experience for athletes worldwide. How It Works

A simple, transparent process to get your custom gear from concept to delivery.

STEP 01

Request a Quote

Fill out our inquiry form with your product type, quantity, and customization requirements. We respond within 24 hours with a detailed quote.

Our design team creates your custom artwork and mockups. Review, revise, and approve your design. We offer unlimited revisions until you're 100% happy.

Before full production, we produce a free physical sample for your approval. Touch and feel the quality yourself before committing to your full order.
